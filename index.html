✦ How It Works

Your Daily Operating System

Five powerful tools that work together to help you find your direction and build momentum.

🤖 Captain AI

Your AI Guide That Plans Your Day

Captain AI analyzes your goals, energy, and schedule to suggest the best path forward. It's not just a task list — it's an intelligent guide that adapts to you.

  • Personalized daily briefings every morning
  • Smart Big Thing suggestions based on your goals
  • Responds in your chosen language instantly
  • Learns your patterns to improve over time
🎯 The 1-3-5 System

One Big Thing. Three Tasks. Five Wins.

The 1-3-5 method is the most effective daily planning framework ever created. NORTH implements it automatically so you always know exactly what to work on.

  • One Big Thing: your most important goal today
  • Three medium tasks that support your Big Thing
  • Five quick wins to build momentum
  • Never feel overwhelmed or directionless again
💰 Safe Number

Know the Number That Sets You Free

Your Safe Number is the monthly income that covers your essential needs and gives you peace of mind. NORTH helps you track it, visualize your progress, and stay motivated.

  • Calculate your personal financial freedom number
  • Track income sources and progress
  • Visual runway calculator and freedom timeline
  • Stay motivated with clear financial milestones
🔥 Momentum Tracking

Track Progress. Build Unstoppable Momentum.

Every win matters. NORTH helps you log achievements, build streaks, and see your momentum grow over time. Small wins compound into extraordinary results.

  • Log wins and celebrate every achievement
  • Streak tracking with visual momentum flame
  • Weekly NORTH Score to measure your progress
  • Shareable achievement cards for social sharing
🌐 Weekly Compass

10-Minute Weekly Reset Ritual

Every Sunday, NORTH guides you through a structured review of your week across five life domains. Reflect, realign, and set your direction for the week ahead.

  • Review five key life domains every week
  • Visual radar chart of your life balance
  • Captain AI insights on your patterns
  • Set intentions for the week ahead
